Here are some of the themes available for Nikola. The images are generated automatically, so
they may not reflect how the sites actually render, and I suggest you see the theme live
at the "preview" link.

Remember that you can install any of these themes with the ``install_theme`` command.

.. class:: alert alert-info 

.. contents:: Available Themes
